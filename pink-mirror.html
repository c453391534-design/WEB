Pink Mirror

Pink Mirror turns your iPhone into a polaroid-style makeup mirror with a built-in inspiration library. Capture references, save the looks you love, and bring them up beside the mirror while you're doing your makeup — all without uploading anything to the cloud.

What it does

  • Mirror view. Front-facing live preview with adjustable zoom (1×, 2×, 4×) and mirror-flip toggle.
  • Reference capture. Take photos or record short clips (up to 10 seconds) as makeup references.
  • Inspiration library. Browse, tag, and pin saved references right next to the mirror.
  • Aspect ratio. Switch between original (9:16) and square (1:1) on the fly.
  • Themes & localization. Light/dark gradient themes, full English & Chinese localization.
  • Offline-first. Everything is stored locally on your device. No account, no cloud sync.

Platform

Built with React Native and Expo. Available on iPhone and iPad. Designed for iOS 17 and above.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Pink Mirror (the "App") handles your information. Pink Mirror is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not operate any backend service that collects, transmits, or stores your personal data.

1. Introduction

Pink Mirror is an iOS application that helps you reference and organize makeup looks. We respect your privacy and have built the App to keep your data on your own device.

2. Information We Collect

Pink Mirror does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers. Specifically:

  • Photos & videos you capture inside the App are saved only to the App's local database on your device.
  • Camera and microphone are used only when you actively take a photo or record a clip inside the App. We do not stream, upload, or share this content.
  • Photo Library access is requested only when you choose to export a reference to your device's Photos. The App does not read or index your existing photo library.
  • We do not collect account information, contact lists, location data, device identifiers, advertising IDs, browsing history, or analytics events.

3. How We Use Information

Because the App does not collect personal information, we do not use your information for any purpose beyond rendering the App on your device. All photos, videos, tags, and preferences remain on your device and are accessible only to you through the App.

4. Third-Party Services

Pink Mirror does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, or social platforms that would collect or transmit your data. The App uses only first-party Apple frameworks (Camera, Photos, AVFoundation) and open-source libraries that run locally on your device.

5. Data Retention

All data you create in Pink Mirror is stored locally in the App's sandboxed storage. You retain full control: you can delete individual items inside the App at any time, or remove all data by uninstalling the App. Uninstalling permanently removes all locally stored content.

6. Children's Privacy

Pink Mirror is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will assist where applicable.

7. Your Rights

Because Pink Mirror does not collect your personal information, there is no remote data for us to access, modify, or delete on your behalf. You have full and direct control over all content created in the App on your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the App or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
