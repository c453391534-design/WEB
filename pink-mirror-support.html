Common Questions

Where is my data stored?
Everything stays on your device. Pink Mirror does not upload your photos, videos, tags, or notes to any server. No accounts, no cloud sync.

How do I delete my data?
Open the Inspiration Library, tap Select / Batch, choose the items you want to remove, and delete them. To remove everything at once, uninstall the app.

The mirror won't open / camera permission was denied.
Open the iOS Settings app → Pink Mirror → toggle Camera and Photos permissions on.

How long can I record?
Up to 10 seconds per video. Long-press the shutter to start; release or wait for the timer to stop.

Why can't I zoom further than 4×?
The iPhone front camera has a hardware zoom ceiling. Pink Mirror gives you the maximum the device can offer while keeping the preview sharp.

常见问题

我的数据保存在哪里？
所有数据都只保存在你的设备上。Pink Mirror 不会把你的照片、视频、标签或文字上传到任何服务器。无需注册，无云同步。

如何删除我的数据？
打开"灵感库"，点击"选择 / 批量"，勾选想删除的内容并删除。如需一次性清空全部，卸载 App 即可。

镜子打不开 / 相机权限被拒绝怎么办？
打开 iOS"设置"App → Pink Mirror → 开启"相机"和"照片"权限。

视频最长能录多久？
每段最长 10 秒。长按快门开始录制，松开或等计时结束即停止。

为什么放大不能超过 4×？
iPhone 前置相机的硬件变焦有上限。Pink Mirror 在保证画面清晰的前提下，提供了设备所能达到的最大缩放。
